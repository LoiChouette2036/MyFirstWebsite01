What is Meditation ?

Meditation is an ancient practice aimed at calming the mind and cultivating mindful presence.

It involves concentrating on an object or focal point, setting aside thoughts and distractions.

The benefits of meditation include reducing stress, improving sleep, and boosting mental clarity.

It can be practiced in different ways and does not require specific religious beliefs.

By practicing meditation regularly, one can develop greater self-awareness and better management of emotions, thus promoting general well-being.
